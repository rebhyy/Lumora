# Lumora - AI Personal Assistant App

![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Active-brightgreen)
![Dart](https://img.shields.io/badge/Dart-100%25-brightgreen)
![Flutter](https://img.shields.io/badge/Flutter-Cross%20Platform-blue)

**Lumora** is a powerful AI-driven personal assistant application built with Dart 3 and Flutter. It combines state-of-the-art AI technologies to provide a comprehensive suite of features that enhance productivity and streamline daily tasks. With integrations from OpenAI's ChatGPT and Google Gemini AI, Lumora offers an intelligent, responsive, and versatile assistant right at your fingertips.

## üöÄ Features

### **AI Chatbot**
- **Technology:** GPT and Google Gemini AI
- **Functionality:** Engage in meaningful conversations, obtain instant answers to queries, and receive tailored recommendations based on your interactions.

### **AI Image Generator**
- **Technology:** GPT and Lexica Image Search
- **Functionality:** Generate high-quality images from text descriptions or existing visuals, perfect for creative projects and social media content.

### **AI Multi-Language Translator**
- **Technology:** GPT and Google Translator
- **Functionality:** Translate text between numerous languages with precision, facilitating seamless communication across different languages.

### **Custom Light/Dark Mode**
- **Functionality:** Choose between light and dark themes to suit your visual preferences and improve readability under various lighting conditions.

### **Onboarding Screens**
- **Functionality:** Provides a user-friendly introduction to the app, ensuring a smooth start for first-time users.

### **Animations**
- **Functionality:** Includes engaging animations such as text effects, Lottie animations, and fading transitions to enhance the app's visual appeal.

### **Modern UI**
- **Technology:** Utilizes new Material widgets
- **Functionality:** Offers a sleek, intuitive interface designed for a pleasant user experience.

### **Cloud Integration**
- **Technology:** App Write
- **Functionality:** Integrates with cloud services for efficient data management and real-time updates.

### **Additional Features**
- **Facebook Ads Integration:** Demonstrates ad capabilities (for sample purposes).
- **And Much More:** Explore additional functionalities and enhancements within the app.
